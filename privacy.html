Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026. This policy explains how Roly Home Services collects, uses, and protects the information you share with us.

1. Information We Collect

When you request a free estimate or contact us, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, phone number, email address, property address, and details about your project. We may also collect basic technical data (such as browser type and pages viewed) to help us improve our website.

2. SMS Messaging & Consent

By submitting our estimate form and providing your phone number, you agree to receive SMS text messages, phone calls, and emails from Roly Home Services regarding your project, scheduling, and follow-up. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. Reply HELP for assistance. Consent to receive messages is not a condition of any purchase. Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to prepare estimates, schedule on-site visits, complete your project, communicate with you, and respond to your questions. We may use it to send service updates, follow-up reminders, and requests for feedback.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers (such as our scheduling and customer-management software) solely to operate our business and serve you. We may disclose information when required by law.

5. Data Retention & Security

We retain your information only as long as needed to provide our services and meet legal obligations. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information, though no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

6. Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you. To make a request, contact us using the details below. You may opt out of SMS by replying STOP and out of email by using the unsubscribe link.
