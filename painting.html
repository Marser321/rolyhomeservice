Meticulous Craftsmanship

Our Specialized Painting Services

We don't cut corners. From detailed prep work to the final clean coat, we treat your property with absolute care.

Interior

Meticulous Interior Painting

We wrap all furniture and floors with double-layer protection, patch and sand drywall to perfection, and apply premium coatings for rich, durable wall and trim colors.

  • Full room masking & wrapping
  • Drywall patching & seamless repair
  • Razor-sharp lines on trim & baseboards
Exterior

Durable Exterior Painting

Siding, trim, fascia, shutters, and doors. We scrape loose paint, caulk seams with premium sealants, and apply weather-resistant coatings to shield your home from Georgia humidity.

  • Mold cleaning & pressure wash prep
  • Caulking & weather sealing protection
  • High-durability weather-shield coatings
Cabinets

High-End Cabinet Painting

Transform your kitchen. We disassemble doors and drawers, sand them flat, apply factory-grade bond primer, and spray-apply a smooth, durable polyurethane enamel.

  • Smooth, factory-grade spray finish
  • Hardware and hinge upgrades
  • Completed in 3-5 days
A System You Can Trust

The Roly Painting Process

Our professional, step-by-step approach ensures premium, long-lasting results and zero hassle for you.

1

Meticulous Prep

We cover all surfaces and execute dust-free sanding and scrubbing. A great paint job is 80% prep, and we ensure the foundation is clean and smooth.

2

Precision Application

We apply premium paints (like Sherwin-Williams Emerald or Behr Marquee) using professional methods, guaranteeing uniform thickness and deep color saturation.

3

Clean-Up & Walkthrough

We remove all tape and plastic, clean the site completely, and walk through the project with you to ensure every line is sharp and every corner looks perfect.
